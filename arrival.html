Bring traveling gear, your creative juices, and an appetite for Awesome™. For workshops and networking, bring a sketchbook, business cards, some writing utensils, your laptop (and CHARGERS), and maybe your camera. If you’re couch surfing, bring a pillow, blanket, and your teddy bear.
